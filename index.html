OptiWeight-PC Decision Aid Tool

Purpose

This tool integrates patient discrete-choice preferences, clinical efficacy data, and cost-effectiveness parameters to evaluate pre-operative weight-loss programmes for men scheduled for robotic-assisted radical prostatectomy.

Use the Inputs tab to customise programme features, then explore predicted uptake, net benefits, ICER distributions, and save scenarios for comparison or export as a PDF.
