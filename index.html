OptiWeight-PC Decision Aid Tool

Purpose

This tool integrates patient preferences, clinical evidence and economic parameters to help patients, clinicians and policy-makers evaluate pre-surgical weight-loss options for obese men scheduled for robotic radical prostatectomy.

Use the Inputs tab to tailor programme characteristics, then explore predicted uptake, net benefits, ICER distributions and save multiple scenarios for comparison.
